Description


The project will protect and help the animals from cruelty by the collected money through a donation drive. The donation drive will be open for all who are willing to donate their money for a cause. They can transfer the money via online banking, Gcash, or Paymaya. The money will use to buy essential things for the animals in the shelter. This project will also launch a website, Email, and a Facebook page.

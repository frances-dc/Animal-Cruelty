Purpose


The main goal of this project is to protect those animals who are forced and abused in malicious activities, bringing harm upon these animals. This project will enable those animals to be free from harm and be taken to nice adoptive homes and animal shelter in where they will be supplied with food and other necessities. It also allows families to adopt the dogs for free.
